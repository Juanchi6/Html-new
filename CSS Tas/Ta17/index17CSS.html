Gonzalo Bergessio

Gonzalo Bergessio is an Argentine striker who left a significant mark at Club Nacional de Football. He joined the club in 2018 and quickly became a key player and team captain. With his character, experience, and goals, he played a crucial role in winning several titles, including Uruguayan championships. He stood out for his commitment, leadership, and strong connection with the fans. In total, he scored 77 official goals wearing Nacional jersey, making him one of the top foreign scorers in the clubs history.
